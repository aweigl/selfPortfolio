About Me

About me:

At the beginning of 2017 was the time, I decided that the field of Biology, which I had studied for 3 years and worked in for 2, was just not right for me anymore. I have always been interested in computers, so the logical consequence was to look into software development. To understand the concepts of programming, I started doing free online development courses, such as Codecademy and Freecodecamp.
That's when I started looking for environments, which would allow me to dive deeper into the fascinating concepts of programming. Due to their popularity and high success-rate in the States, I searched for coding boot camps in Germany. After doing some research, I decided on SPICED academy. A coding boot camp, located in Berlin. In an intensive 12 Week Full Stack Development course, students were taught the fundamentals as well as top of the line programming technologies including ECMAScript6, CSS3, HTML5, Node.js and React.
